        Help
        Hotkeys
        • Space/→: Step one bar
        • F: Fast forward 24 bars, or until triggers an alert/order
        • Z/←: Stepback one bar
        • Hover over the charts:
          • A: Alert
          • B: Buy order
          • S: Sell order
          • D: Display info panel
          • G: Go to hovered time
        • Scales:
          • Q: Fit to left chart
          • W/E: Fit to right chart
          • R: Reset all scales
